FAQ

Answers to common questions about Jengaflow.

How does Jengaflow ensure data security?

Jengaflow uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect all user data. We also implement strict access controls and regular security audits.

Is Jengaflow available outside of Kenya?

While our initial focus is on the Kenyan market, we plan to expand to other East African countries and eventually globally.

How does the AI planning feature work?

Our AI planning tool uses machine learning algorithms to analyze project requirements and generate optimized construction plans and architectural designs.
