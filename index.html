Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Jengaflow.

How does Jengaflow ensure data security?

Jengaflow uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect all user data. We also implement strict access controls and regular security audits to ensure the highest level of data protection.

Is Jengaflow available outside of Kenya?

While our initial focus is on the Kenyan market, we plan to expand to other East African countries and eventually globally. Our platform is designed to be adaptable to different construction industries and regulations.

How does the AI planning feature work?

Our AI planning tool uses machine learning algorithms to analyze project requirements and generate optimized construction plans and architectural designs. It takes into account factors like budget, timeline, materials, and local regulations to create efficient project plans.
